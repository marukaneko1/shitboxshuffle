Boilerplate (short)

Shitbox Shuffle is a US-only, adults-only platform that combines random 1:1 video matching and a public game lobby with real multiplayer titles and optional token-based play—positioned as a structured alternative to legacy anonymous roulette chat.

Fact sheet

  • Product: Web app — matching (“Shuffle Live”), lobby rooms, in-session games, accounts, wallet/tokens per Terms.
  • Audience: United States residents, 18+, identity/age verification as described in product flow.
  • Positioning: “Meet a stranger. Play a game. Put something on it.” — random social video + games + stakes, not a kids’ chat site.
  • Founding story angle: Post-2023 Omegle shutdown; demand for alternatives that add structure (games) and clearer adult eligibility.
