What Monkey is

Monkey is a random video chat app available on iOS and Android. It launched as a direct competitor to Omegle targeting a younger demographic, with a TikTok-style aesthetic and social features including friend requests and connection tracking.

The app was positioned around the "meet someone random and maybe become friends" premise, with short initial video segments and the option to extend if both users are interested—a swipe-right mechanic applied to stranger video.

Age rating and actual audience

This is the most important thing to understand about Monkey before using it:

  • Monkey's stated minimum age varies by platform and has changed over time
  • The app has been rated 17+ at times and required parental consent features at other points
  • The actual user base is reported to skew significantly toward teenagers and young adults (Gen Z)
  • Monkey has been specifically featured in teen-safety discussions by parental organisations, which is an indicator of its actual audience demographics

For adults specifically looking for other adults: the Monkey audience is not primarily adults, and there is no hard verification mechanism that changes this. This is not speculation—it reflects the platform's positioning, marketing, and the demographic discussions that have accompanied it in the press.

Features

  • Random video matching with a short initial clip before the user decides to extend
  • Friend request functionality for connections you want to keep
  • Text and emoji reactions during video
  • Social graph features (mutual friends, network connections)
  • Mobile-only (iOS and Android)
  • No built-in games or structured activity layer
  • No wagering or token system

Safety concerns and history

Monkey has faced safety scrutiny as a platform attracting minors:

  • Multiple consumer safety organisations have published concerns about Monkey specifically in the context of minor-to-adult interaction risks
  • The platform has made policy changes in response to these concerns, but the fundamental issue—a young demographic without hard age verification—remains structural
  • Like all random video platforms, Monkey has no technical mechanism to prevent screen recording
  • Standard random video chat safety practices apply, but the age demographic concern makes this platform higher-risk for adults who don't want to interact with minors
