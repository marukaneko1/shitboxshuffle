What random video chat is

At core, it is live audiovisual pairing: you connect to another person (or a small group) through a browser or app, usually without a long onboarding flow. Some products are pure roulette—tap “next” and you see someone new. Others add interests, karma scores, payments, or shared activities.

Risks nobody should ignore

  • Recording — assume the other side can capture the stream.
  • Scams — phishing links, “move to another app,” crypto pitches.
  • Harassment — instant exit + report; do not negotiate.
  • Age mixing — adult services exist precisely to avoid minor traffic; still verify each product’s rules.

Etiquette that helps

Consent still matters on stranger video: ask before sensitive topics, respect immediate “no,” and keep the camera appropriate for a public-facing product’s rules. Good etiquette raises the baseline for everyone in the pool.

Tech checklist

Allow camera/mic only for domains you trust. Use updated browsers. If latency is high, wired internet beats Wi‑Fi. For competitive games, low ping matters—see our Room when you want video + matchmaking in one stack.
