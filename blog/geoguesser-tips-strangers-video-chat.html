GeoGuessr-style tips for stranger video matches

March 2026 · General tactics; in-game rules always apply

Speed-read the frame

In live rounds you rarely get infinite panning. Scan skyline, road width, vegetation zone, and signage language in the first pass. Narrow to continent before you chase a country.

Road DNA

Center-line color, reflector posts, guardrail shapes, and roundabout style differ by region. EU vs Americas vs Oceania often separate within seconds once you’ve practiced.

Vegetation & climate

Palm vs pine vs savanna grass, snow line, irrigation patterns—cheap heuristics that beat random clicking.
