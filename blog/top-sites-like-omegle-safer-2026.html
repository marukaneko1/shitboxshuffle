Top 10 sites like Omegle—what “safer” can (and can’t) mean

March 2026 · Editorial · Adults should verify every site’s current policies

Reality check: “Safe” is not a binary. No operator can promise zero harm on live video. Use report/block, never share passwords or payment info on camera, and assume recording is possible. This list reflects what people search for, not an endorsement of every product’s enforcement.
