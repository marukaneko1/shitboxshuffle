How we ranked these

After Omegle shut down in 2023, search demand for “Omegle alternative” exploded. This list is optimized for readers who want clarity, not hype:

  • Intent match — Does the product actually offer random or semi-random video, or is it a different social pattern?
  • Structure — Open-ended roulette vs. profiles, interests, or shared activities (like games).
  • US / adult context — We call out 18+ positioning where relevant; minors should not use adult-marketed services.

Third-party sites change policies often—verify on their official pages before signing up.
