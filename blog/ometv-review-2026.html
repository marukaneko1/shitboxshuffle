What OmeTV is

OmeTV is a random video chat platform that launched as a mobile-first alternative to Omegle and Chatroulette. It operates globally across iOS, Android, and browser, with a user base concentrated in South America, Southeast Asia, Eastern Europe, and North America.

The basic format is the same as Chatroulette: connect to a random stranger, see each other live, skip to the next if you want. OmeTV differentiates with a gender filter (premium), country filter (premium), and a coin-based currency for accessing certain features.

App vs browser

OmeTV's strongest product is its mobile app. The iOS and Android apps are well-optimised compared to competitors and represent the clearest use case for the platform—random video on mobile, on the go.

The browser version is functional but feels secondary to the mobile experience. If you're using OmeTV on desktop, the experience is adequate but not differentiated from other browser-based alternatives.

Key consideration: the app's 12+ age rating on major app stores means children can download and access the platform with no additional friction beyond whatever parental controls their device has.

Moderation and safety

  • Gender filter: Available (requires coins). Lets you specify who you want to match with. The filter isn't perfectly accurate—accounts can misrepresent gender—but it's better than pure random.
  • AI moderation: Content detection is in place for NSFW content. Effectiveness is reported as inconsistent, particularly during peak hours.
  • Report system: In-session reporting is available. Response and action speed are not publicly disclosed.
  • Age verification: Terms say 18+. App store rating says 12+. There is no hard verification mechanism. The actual audience age distribution is genuinely mixed.
  • Bots: Users report above-average bot and promotional account rates. Platform claims to address this but it remains a reported issue.
  • Account requirement: Basic use doesn't require an account. Premium features do. This limits the effectiveness of bans for persistent bad actors.

FAQ

Is OmeTV free?

Basic access is free. Coins are required for gender and country filters. There may be premium subscription options—check the current app listing for up-to-date pricing.

What's the OmeTV age limit?

OmeTV's Terms of Service state 18+. The app store ratings are 12+. There is no hard age verification mechanism. The effective minimum age is whatever a user's guardian allows.

Does OmeTV work in the US?

Yes, OmeTV works in the US and globally. US users are in the matching pool along with users from all other accessible countries.
