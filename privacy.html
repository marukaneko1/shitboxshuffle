Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Shitbox Shuffle respects your privacy. We collect only what we need to run the Service and never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, username, age confirmation, and state of residence.

Identity Verification: To comply with US regulations and enable withdrawals, we may collect government-issued ID information processed by a third-party KYC provider.

Financial Information: Payment method details are processed and stored by our payment processor. We do not store full card numbers or bank account details on our servers.

Usage Data: We collect game session data (match results, wager history, game choices) to operate the Service and detect fraud.

Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system for security and abuse prevention.

2. Camera & Video

Shitbox Shuffle requires camera access for live 1:1 video matching. Your video stream is transmitted in real-time to your matched opponent only. We do not record, store, or analyze your video feed.

Camera access is controlled by your browser permissions. You can revoke access at any time in your browser settings, though doing so will prevent you from participating in matches.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process wagers, payouts, and refunds
  • To match you with other players via our random pairing system
  • To detect and prevent fraud, cheating, and abuse
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To send you transactional emails (match results, withdrawal confirmations)

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Payment processors to handle deposits and withdrawals
  • KYC/identity verification providers as required for compliance
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting, database) bound by data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement when required by valid legal process

5. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active. Financial records are retained for 7 years as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting Support.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit (TLS), hashed password storage, and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure — use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication when available.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us via Support.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notice. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
